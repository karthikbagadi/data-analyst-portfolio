# E-commerce Sales Analysis

## Objective

Analyze sales data to identify revenue trends, top products, and top-performing cities.

## Tools Used

* Excel
* Pivot Tables

## Key Insights

* Best Month: April (Highest revenue)
* Top Product: MacBook Pro Laptop
* Top City: San Francisco

## Conclusion

High-value products and metro cities contribute most to revenue. Business can focus more on these areas to maximize profit.
